UWalk

U Walk is Laurier's first charity walking club. We aim to learn, inspire and educate by promoting healthy living & spreading Golden Hawk spirit!

  • Charities We Support

Canadian Cancer Society

The Canadian Cancer Society is a national, community based organization of volunteers whose mission is the eradication of cancer and the enhancement of the quality of life of people living with cancer.

  • Learn more

Heart and Stroke Foundation

The Heart and Stroke Foundation is a national voluntary non-profit organization whose mission is to improve the health of Canadians by preventing and deducing disability and death from heart disease and stroke through research, health promotion and advocacy.

  • Learn more

SickKids Foundation

SickKids Foundation works to inspire communities to invest in health and scientific advances to imporve the lives of children and their familes in Canada and around the world.
